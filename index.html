About us

Who can Invest?

At LPC, we’re very particular about who we allow to invest. We achieve some of the best returns in industry, so we ensure that we only take capital from investors who go through our vetting process.

At a minimum, we require accreditation status. We then have an onboarding questionnaire for all investors. Once completed, those who pass then go through a short interview, after which we notify investors of their approval status.
